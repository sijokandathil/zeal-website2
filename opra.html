Introducing OPRA, The New Standard for Overseas Pharmacist Assessments

Starting March 2025, the Knowledge Assessmentof Pharmaceutical Sciences(KAPS) exam will be succeeded by the Overseas Pharmacist Readiness Assessment (OPRA) exam. The Australian Pharmacy Council will discontinue the KAPS exam after November 2024. This change reflects the Council's commitment to advancing exam standards and maintaining alignment with contemporary Australian pharmacy practices. Conducted by the Australian & New Zealand Pharmacy Council, this exam is a pivotal step to becoming a registered pharmacist in Australia.

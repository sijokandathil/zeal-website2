Our Dedicated & Experienced Team

Dr. Anphil Thomas, Pharm. D

Founder & Managing Director

Dr. Anphil Thomas, the visionary behind Zeal Academy, is a distinguished educator and innovator in the pharmaceutical field, inspiring aspiring pharmacists since 2017. He excelled in the Australian Pharmacy Council's KAPS exam shortly after his graduation. His leadership trancend traditional teaching, guiding students towards success in foreign pharmacy licensure exams with unparalleled expertise and mentorship. At Zeal Academy, under his guidance, students cultivate a mindset of growth and exploration, preparing to create lasting impacts in the dynamic healthcare industry.

Dr. Lithin Philiph, Pharm. D

Co-Founder & Pharmacy Manager (Registered Pharmacict), Terrywhite Chenmart, Australia

Dr. Lithin is a Registered pharmacist in Australia, who began his career as an international exam instructor following his success in KAPS examination. After completing his internship and passing both the written and oral intern exams, he earned his registration as a pharmacist. Renowned for his expertise, Dr. Lithin excels in an instructor and is committed to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Ms. Anjusha Jose M

Visa Operations Director

As the director of visa operations at Zeal Academy, she adeptly manages both the academy's operational and visa functions. Her leadership is characterised by a positive demeanour and a thoughtful approach to addressing candidates' needs. Specialising in guiding pharmacists through the internship process in Australia, she leverages her extensive expertise to facilitate placements across key states, including new South Wales, Victoria, South Australia and Queensland. The ability to merge strategic decision-making with genuine care highlights her commitment to driving success and fostering a supportive and professional environment.
